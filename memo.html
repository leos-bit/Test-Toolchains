Subject: Portfolio Design and Content Rationale

Target Employer and Key Skills

I am targeting roles in semiconductor manufacturing & design, robotics startups, and physical AI teams. These employers value systems thinking, reliable deployment, and evidence of end-to-end ownership. The portfolio foregrounds mechatronics integration, data-driven diagnostics, embedded controls, and applied ML.

Content Choices

I selected four projects/experiences that demonstrate technical depth and social value: semiconductor tool modernization (improving uptime and yield), a solar-powered robot (energy-aware autonomy), an AI game agent (learning under uncertainty), and connected mobility systems. Each entry follows an IMRD/IDEC structure and explicitly names my contributions.

Audience Alignment

For employers focused on reliability and throughput, I emphasize measurable outcomes (uptime, downtime, stability). For robotics and physical AI teams, I highlight kinematic design, controls, and learning systems. The layout makes skills quickly scannable through project headings, metrics, and tags.

Design Rationale

The design prioritizes clarity and fast navigation. A clean minimal layout, high-contrast typography, and generous whitespace improve readability. Cards separate projects to reduce cognitive load. Consistent navigation eliminates broken paths, while the resume and memo are placed in dedicated pages for quick access.

Future Enhancements

I plan to add demo videos of the terrain walker and tooling dashboards, and include a short project summary video for rapid review.
